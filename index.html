About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Carlos Petit What we do is the testimony of who we are. People can be involved or not, but for me it is a matter of respect for others. Not getting involved in the work is simply an offense.

Make each product as best as possible, demonstrating and pushing my capabilities to the limit. Focused on the form and the tools. What we do not include is as important as what we include. The goal is clear, and it is not making money. The goal that has to be pursued desperately is to make the best possible product. If you trust that you have achieved it, people will like it, buy it, and enjoy using it.

I'm not afraid of failing. If we do not fail, it is that we are not trying hard enough. 80% of the things that I develop in the study are not good enough. If after trying my best I do not think it's good enough, I just stop working on it and continue the iteration process until I get a product that is of sufficient quality.
